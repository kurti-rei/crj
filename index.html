About the Journal

CIT Review Journal (CRJ) (ISSN: 2960-2831) is an international, double-blind peer-reviewed, open access journal serving as a platform for publishing original research in the fields of Economics, Business, and Information Technology. CRJ actively encourages interdisciplinary submissions bridging business, economics, cybersecurity, data science, and artificial intelligence, shedding light on their impacts on management, organizations, and society.

Published bi-annually (May and November) by the Canadian Institute of Technology (CIT), Tirana, Albania. All articles are freely available online immediately upon publication under a CC BY 4.0 license.

Aims and Scope

Aims

CIT Review Journal (CRJ) ISSN: 2960-2831 is an international, peer-reviewed, open access journal (free for readers) that serves as a platform for publishing original research in the fields of Economics, Business, and Information Technology. CRJ actively encourages submissions that integrate interdisciplinary research from domains like business, economics, cybersecurity, data science, and artificial intelligence, shedding light on their impacts on management, organizations, and society at large. In order to uphold its standards, CRJ conducts prompt and thorough reviews in accordance with accepted publication procedures.

Scope

Economics and Business

  • Macroeconomics and microeconomics
  • Financial markets and institutions
  • International trade and globalization
  • Entrepreneurship and innovation
  • Marketing, management, and organizational behavior
  • Accounting and auditing
  • Human resource management
  • Strategic management and corporate governance

Information Technology

  • Artificial intelligence and machine learning
  • Cybersecurity and information security
  • Data science and big data analytics
  • Software engineering and systems design
  • Internet of Things (IoT)
  • Cloud computing and distributed systems
  • Digital transformation

Interdisciplinary Research

CRJ particularly welcomes manuscripts that bridge business and technology, demonstrating how digital innovations transform economic systems, organizational structures, and societal well-being.

Home/Vision and Mission

Vision and Mission

Vision

To be a leading international academic journal recognized for excellence in disseminating high-quality, interdisciplinary research that bridges the fields of Economics, Business, and Information Technology — contributing to knowledge creation, innovation, and sustainable development in Albania and the Western Balkan region.

Mission

The mission of CIT Review Journal is to provide a rigorous, peer-reviewed platform for researchers, academics, practitioners, and policymakers to share original and impactful research. CRJ is committed to:

  • Promoting open access to scientific knowledge
  • Supporting emerging researchers and scholars from the Western Balkans and beyond
  • Facilitating cross-disciplinary dialogue between economics, business, and technology
  • Upholding the highest standards of academic integrity and ethical publication
  • Contributing to the evidence base for policy and decision-making

Journal History

CIT Review Journal (CRJ) was established by the Canadian Institute of Technology (CIT) in Tirana, Albania, as part of the institution's commitment to advancing academic research and scholarship in the region.

Milestones

  • 2014 — CRJ founded as the official academic journal of the Canadian Institute of Technology
  • 2019 — First international issues published with scholars from the Western Balkans
  • 2022 — Journal relaunch with bi-annual publication schedule (May and November)
  • 2023 — DOI assignment system introduced; journal pursued academic database indexing
  • 2024 — ISSN 2960-2831 officially registered; editorial board expanded internationally
  • 2025 — Growing international authorship across 10+ countries
  • 2026 — May 2026 Issue published; Scopus indexing application in progress

Submit a Manuscript

CRJ welcomes original research articles, review papers, and case studies in Economics, Business, and Information Technology. All submissions undergo double-blind peer review.

1

Prepare

Read the Author Instructions and use the official CRJ paper template.

2

Submit

Send your manuscript as PDF and Word files to the editorial office by email.

3

Review

Your paper undergoes double-blind peer review. Average time: 4–6 weeks.

Submission Guidelines

  • Manuscripts must be original and not under review elsewhere
  • Papers must follow the CRJ template and formatting requirements
  • Include a structured abstract (max 250 words) and 5–8 keywords
  • References should follow APA or IEEE format as applicable
  • Submissions: crj@cit.edu.al with subject "Manuscript Submission – [Paper Title]"
Submission Email: crj@cit.edu.al
Home/Instructions for Authors

Instructions for Authors

Manuscript Preparation

  • Length: Research articles: 4,000–8,000 words; Review articles: up to 10,000 words
  • Font: Times New Roman, 12pt, double-spaced
  • Margins: 2.5 cm on all sides
  • File format: Microsoft Word (.docx) and PDF

Structure

  • Title, author names and affiliations, abstract, keywords
  • Introduction, Literature Review, Methodology, Results, Discussion, Conclusion
  • References (APA or IEEE format)
  • Appendices (if applicable)

Abstract

The abstract should be 150–250 words and structured to include: objective, methods, results, and conclusions. Do not include citations in the abstract.

Keywords

Provide 5–8 keywords that accurately describe the paper's subject matter.

Figures and Tables

All figures and tables must be numbered, titled, and cited in the text. Figures should be provided at minimum 300 DPI resolution.

Ethics Statement

Authors must confirm that the research was conducted ethically and that all human/animal studies complied with applicable regulations.

Home/Peer Review Policy

Peer Review Policy

CRJ uses a rigorous double-blind peer review process. All submitted manuscripts that pass initial editorial screening are evaluated by at least two independent expert reviewers.

1

Submission

Author submits manuscript to editorial office by email.

2

Editor Screen

Editor-in-Chief reviews for scope and basic quality. Desk rejection possible.

3

Peer Review

Sent to 2+ anonymous reviewers. Authors also anonymous (double-blind).

Review Criteria

  • Originality and novelty of the research
  • Scientific rigor and methodological soundness
  • Relevance to the journal's scope
  • Clarity and quality of writing
  • Adequate referencing and citation

Timeline

Authors can typically expect an initial decision within 4–6 weeks of submission. Revised manuscripts are generally reviewed within 2–3 weeks.

Home/Plagiarism Statement

Plagiarism Statement

CIT Review Journal has a zero-tolerance policy for plagiarism. All submissions are screened for plagiarism prior to peer review using industry-standard detection software.

Definition

  • Copying text from other sources without proper attribution
  • Paraphrasing another's work without citation
  • Self-plagiarism (reusing one's own previously published work without disclosure)
  • Data fabrication or falsification

Consequences

Manuscripts found to contain plagiarism will be immediately rejected. If plagiarism is detected post-publication, the article will be retracted and the authors' institutions notified.

Acceptable Similarity

A similarity index of up to 15% (excluding references) may be acceptable, provided matched sections are properly attributed. Final judgment rests with the Editor-in-Chief.
